The Fucking Best Vegan G.B.C (Green Bean Casserole)

Description

This is how to make the best fucking lasagna without the need for gross cheese and meat. Fuck carnivores.

This will include cashews, tofu, lemon, and fresh herbs along with what you would expect in a lasagna recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups soaked cashews
  • 1 large jar of sauce (prefer Raos)
  • Noodles (the kind that bake in the oven)
  • 2 bricks of frim tofu (pressed)
  • 2 Lemons
  • Herbs
  • other shit

Steps

  1. Soak dem nuts for at least a few hours
  2. Blend nuts and tofu adding herbs, olive oil, and salt + pepper to taste.
  3. Ensure ricotta like consistency.
